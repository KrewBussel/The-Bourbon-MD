Our History

2015

The Beginning

The Bourbon MD was born from a shared passion between two friends who dreamed of creating a space where bourbon enthusiasts could gather, learn, and enjoy premium spirits in a sophisticated setting.

2017

First Location

We opened our doors in the heart of Louisville, Kentucky, with a carefully curated selection of 50 bourbons and a commitment to exceptional service.

2019

Expansion

Our collection grew to over 100 bourbons, and we introduced our premium cigar selection, creating the perfect pairing experience for our guests.

2022

VIP Program

Launched our exclusive VIP membership program, offering our most dedicated patrons access to rare bottles, private events, and personalized experiences.
